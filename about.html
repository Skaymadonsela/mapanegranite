About Us

Established in 2008 and headquartered in Gauteng, we offer a diverse range of products and services. Specializing in designing and installing kitchens and vanities for new homes, renovations, and offices, our product lineup includes granite quartz, Caesarstone, Dekton, Infinity Porcelain, and marble slabs.

With an average of 15 years of experience, our skilled artisans bring unparalleled expertise to each project, ensuring the highest standards of craftsmanship and customer satisfaction

Our Mission

Our mission is to realize our client's vision, one project at a time, ensuring their dreams are brought to life with precision and excellence.

Our Vision

At Mapane, our unwavering commitment to exceptional quality drives our vision. We aspire to become the premier choice for clients looking to revamp their spaces, ensuring we're their first and best option.

Why choose us?

Our ability to bring outstanding results to our customers.
